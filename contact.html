Call forEditorialBoard Membership

MJRET invites Academicians and Corporates to join us as a Member of the Editorial Board. Being a Member of the Editorial Board is a matter of prestige and personnel achievement. The minimum qualification required to become a Member of the Editorial Board is:

  • PhD degree holders of any dicispline from a recognized university.
  • Research author with a 3(Three) MJRET publication.
  • Author should have 3(Three) publications in reputed journals.
  • Corporate employees having more than 2 years of experinece.

Applicants can download the Application Form from DOWNLOAD SECTION, fill the application form and send us at Chief Editor
Editor in Chief will revert back to the applicant within 2 to 3 working days. The whole process will take around a weeks time.
